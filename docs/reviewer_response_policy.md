# Reviewer Response Policy

v0.4.0 supports anticipated reviewer-objection planning, not fabricated response letters.

Rules:

- Use `reviewer_objection_map` for likely risks only.
- Do not write `Reviewer 1 said`, `Reviewer 2 asked`, or equivalent wording unless the user supplies real reviewer comments.
- Every response strategy must point to a manuscript repair, evidence need, experiment need, or explicit no-change rationale.
- High or critical objections require a response strategy before manuscript readiness can pass.
- Response plans must preserve uncertainty when citations or results are missing.
